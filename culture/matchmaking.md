# Arranged marriage & matchmaking — रिश्ता / कुंडली मिलान

READ before writing/rendering any match-arranging, rishta, "seeing the girl/boy," engagement, dowry, or
photo/proposal scene. *(v1 draft — correct me.)*

## How it works
- Marriages are **arranged by families**, not chosen by the couple. A match comes through relatives, a पंडित,
  or a **नाई / मध्यस्थ** (broker). The young people usually don't court.
- **Gates on a match:** same **caste/community (जाति/समाज)**, **different गोत्र** (gotra exogamy), the **boy's
  job/prospects**, and **हैसियत** — the families' standing and wealth.
- **कुंडली मिलान** (horoscope match): a pandit matches birth-charts — **गुण मिलान** (out of 36 गुण; ~18+ needed),
  checks **मांगलिक / मंगल दोष**. A bad reading can sink a rishta.
- **Process:** biodata/**photo exchange** → families meet, **"लड़की / लड़का देखना"** (the boy's side comes to
  *see* the girl, usually at her home, tea served, she's shown) → **रोका** (informal lock) → **सगाई** (engagement).

## Dowry (दहेज) — the open secret
Legally banned (Dowry Prohibition Act) but **pervasive**. The **girl's family "gives"** cash, gold, a vehicle
(a Bullet/motorcycle, a car), furniture, household goods to the **boy's side**. It is NEVER called dowry openly
— it's **रिवाज़ / लेन-देन / शगुन / "gifts."** The boy's side often **demands**; the girl's side strains to meet it.
A debt-burdened family may marry a daughter against the debt.

## The girl's place
- **Hypergamy:** a girl is married "**up**" — to a wealthier/older man if it raises the family or clears a debt.
- **Her consent is traditionally not sought.** She is **"given" (कन्यादान)**; her wishes are secondary, often
  unspoken. Refusing a fixed match is a scandal.
- Girls married young; a much-older groom or a second marriage is accepted for **हैसियत / money**.

## Rules (avoid violations)
Don't show the couple freely choosing each other in this milieu; the girl's wishes are secondary; **dowry is
"gifts," never named**; **kundli / gotra / caste / हैसियत gate the match**; the boy's side is courted, the girl
"shown."

## Dramatic hooks (AMAL)
The photo/rishta; **Leela priced against Bherulal's debt, married to old Dhanraj**; **Manju's silent
non-consent**; **Deva's family straining under dowry demands** — the same market that prices the dead girl.
