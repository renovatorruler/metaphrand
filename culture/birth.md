# Birth rites — जन्म संस्कार

READ before writing/rendering any birth, newborn, naming, or छठी scene. *(v1 draft — correct me.)*

## Rules
- A birth brings **सूतक** (birth impurity, ~10–11 days) on the family — like death-sutak: restricted activity,
  the house "unclean," until शुद्धि. The mother is in **confinement (जापा)** ~40 days, special diet, cared for.
- **Strong SON-preference (patrilineal).** A son carries the **name/lineage**, lights the parents' pyre,
  inherits the land/पट्टा; a **son's birth is celebrated loudly** (sweets, dhol, distribution); a **daughter's
  quietly**, sometimes as a burden (future dowry). Female feticide/infanticide has a real history in this belt.

## Sequence
**जातकर्म** (at birth) → **नामकरण** (naming ~11th day, by a pandit per the नक्षत्र/राशि) → **छठी** (6th-day rite) →
निष्क्रमण (first outing) → **अन्नप्राशन** (first solid food ~6 months) → **मुंडन** (see mundan.md).

## Dramatic hooks (AMAL)
The whole **"पट्टा बड़ा या बेटा"** engine — a four-year-old son's name on the licence is worth a dead daughter;
sons celebrated, daughters priced. A daughter's birth met with silence.
