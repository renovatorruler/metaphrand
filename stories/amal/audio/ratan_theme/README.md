## CORE phrase (constant in every prompt — reuse to re-cut or extend)
> Ratan's theme — the recurring leitmotif of a heavy, weary, honourable Malwa police inspector in a tragic
> crime drama. The signature is a single grave, slow descending sarangi phrase that falls and hangs on an
> unresolved note. Solo sarangi lead over a tanpura drone, North Indian acoustic palette, a real melodic theme.

## Placement
One per episode as his theme song (his entrance / an end-card statement). **Ep1 must carry it** (user). Ep2's
*stirring* version can double as the Ratan cue in the Ep2 score (Sc6 entrance, Sc39 final walk) so the
underscore and the theme song share DNA.

## Register / held cards
Weary-tragic, never cheerful. Ep6 is **defiant, not victorious** — the energy of a man who has *chosen*, not
won. Held: saka = Ratan's death in Ep6; the theme rises into defiance, it must not telegraph the death.
