CANON — the established world; flag contradictions:
- Setting: the Malwa opium belt of Madhya Pradesh (Mandsaur/Neemuch), a government opium-licence economy.
- The 'patta' is the licence to grow opium; losing it is ruin; it is inherited and makes the family's marriages.
- Ratan Singh Panwar: a compromised, weary CBN (Central Bureau of Narcotics) inspector, ~52; not an upright hero.
- Bherulal: Leela's father, a licensed grower. Leela: ~16, found dead in the family's poppy field at dawn during lancing (luaai); engaged to a ~40-year-old man for an alliance.
- Sugna: Leela's mother. Deva: a young constable under Ratan.
- Register: gritty realist (Paatal Lok / Aranyak); English sluglines and action, Hindi/Devanagari dialogue.
