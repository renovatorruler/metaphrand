# अमल — The Train, whole (then sliced)

*This is the entire event, built as one continuous scene so it can be seen whole. In the show it is
NEVER shown whole — it is carved into four cold opens spread across the season (cut-points marked in
the `>` notes). Shown, never explained: no caption, no date, her face never clear, the inside of the
bundle never seen. The green cloth, the flat hand, the hummed tune, the flies — these are the rhymes
that return around Sugna in the present, so the viewer makes the join the script never does.*

---

## EXT. JUNCTION PLATFORM — DAY

A small railway platform under a hard sun, crowded the way these platforms are — families on bedrolls,
a tea stall, coolies, a loudspeaker no one listens to. Among them a WOMAN stands near the edge with a
baby held high against her shoulder, wrapped to the crown in a faded green cloth. We see her from the
side and from behind, never quite her face. One hand stays flat on the child's back. When the train
comes in she does not push for it; she waits, and lets the crowd carry her up into a compartment.

## INT. GENERAL COMPARTMENT — DAY

Packed wooden benches, tin trunks, a man asleep sitting upright. The woman takes a place by the barred
window and settles the baby against her, the green cloth at its head. She hums, low, the same few notes
over and over, and lets the train rock her.

A vendor edges through with tea. Across the bench a WOMAN PASSENGER with two children of her own
watches the bundle, friendly.

WOMAN PASSENGER: कितने महीने का है?

The woman at the window does not turn. She draws the cloth up an inch, over the child's head.

WOMAN AT WINDOW: सो रहा है।

The passenger smiles and goes back to her own children. The field runs past the window, the same cut
stubble over and over, and the heat sits in the carriage and does not move.

A fly settles on the green cloth. The woman does not lift her hand to brush it off.

> **CUT — END OF THE EP2 COLD OPEN.** Black, then the title. We have a woman, a sleeping baby, a fly.
> Nothing is wrong yet, and everything is. No one in the carriage has moved, and the audience has no
> idea what they have just watched.

## INT. GENERAL COMPARTMENT — DAY (LATER, OR THE SAME — UNSAID)

The same compartment, the same heat, the field still going past. The woman hums and keeps her hand on
the child's back. There are more flies now, two and then three on the green cloth, and she lets them
sit.

Across the bench the friendly passenger has gone quiet. She is watching the bundle, because the child
has not stirred once in a long time, not at the whistle, not at the vendor's shout, not at anything.
She looks at the man beside her. He has seen it too.

The quiet spreads down the compartment, one face turning and then the next, people looking and then
carefully not looking at the woman by the window, who hums her few notes and watches the stubble go
past. A man near the door gets to his feet. He says something low to another man, and the word goes
from them toward the vestibule, and forward, out of the carriage.

> **CUT — END OF THE EP3 COLD OPEN.** The dread is loose in the carriage and someone has finally moved,
> but nothing has happened yet. By now the present has begun to rhyme with this without a word said.

## INT. GENERAL COMPARTMENT — DAY

The woman at the window keeps humming. Her hand keeps moving on the child's back, slow, the same as it
has the whole way. She does not look at the men by the door. She looks at the field. For one moment the
humming stops, and then it goes on.

## EXT. SMALL STATION — DAY

The train slows along a low platform in the middle of nowhere and stops. Two POLICEMEN are already
there. They come down the platform at a walk that turns into a hurry and swing up into the carriage.

## INT. GENERAL COMPARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

The policemen push down the aisle through the standing crowd. The passengers press back into each other
to make the lane. We stay close on the woman at the window: the flat hand, the green cloth, the small
still shape inside it. A policeman reaches in for the bundle.

> **CUT — END OF THE EP4 COLD OPEN.** The hand reaching the cloth, and black — cutting at the same beat
> the floor drops out in the present-day case. Ep5 holds its own gut-punch and gives the train a rest.

## INT. GENERAL COMPARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

The policeman's hands close on the bundle. The woman's hand holds it down for a moment, then lets go.

We never see her face, and we never see inside the cloth.

## EXT. SMALL STATION — DAY

They bring her down onto the platform between two constables, her hands empty now, the bundle carried
apart from her in a man's two hands. The compartment windows are full of faces. The guard's whistle
goes. The train takes up its weight and pulls away, and the seat by the window where she sat is already
taken by someone else. Beyond the rails the field goes on the same in every direction.

> **THE EVENT COMPLETES — PLACED IN EP6, AT THE REVEAL.** This last piece runs at the instant the
> present-day truth lands (Sugna's hand, and why). The boy and Leela, the two opium-children, close over
> Ratan's whole life in the same breath. Her face is never shown, even here; the join to Sugna is made
> only by the green cloth, the flat hand, the hum, the flies.

**CUT TO BLACK.**
