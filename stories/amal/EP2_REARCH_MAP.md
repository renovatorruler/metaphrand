## The seven moves (the gap)

1. **Add the opening hook.** The spine opens on Dr. Bhanwar handing Ratan the "fall" over **tea** (amal is Rana's, not the doctor's), and Ratan reading it in three seconds (same-day stamp, the slipper flung too far, the wrong angle). The 6.5 has no hook — it opens mid-body-claim at the morgue. → one NEW scene at the front.
2. **Stop Sugna nagging the center.** sc4 (Sugna confronts Ratan) and sc8 (Sugna confronts Bherulal) are the "Sugna keeps bitching" problem. Shrink her to edge-presence — the dry eyes, the steel glass cooling — not center-stage confrontations.
3. **Make the night Run the centerpiece.** Today it's one thin scene (sc19: a tractor across a culvert + a Bolero). The spine wants the spine of the hour: the **boss on the phone** mid-run, the belt's own trap (a night **convoy checkpoint** or a **Muharram tajiya** procession out of Jaora), the Bhil at the dawn field-edge — the place doing the work. → biggest single expansion.
4. **Purge the sister.** The 6.5 still says Leela is Ratan's **sister's** child — sc36 (Kanta: "बहन की बच्ची"), sc38 ("उसकी बहन का घर"), the recoil in sc30. New canon: Sugna is a stranger to Ratan; his stake is the awakening, not blood. Cut every sister reference; the theory points at **Bherulal's** house, not a sister's.
5. **Re-aim the ending to the double doorway.** The 6.5 ends on Ratan alone walking to the (sister's) house. The spine ends on **two men, two secrets**: Ratan's ground gone *and* **Deva discovering Manju is pregnant**. → add the pregnancy button; pair it with Ratan's final walk.
6. **Reframe the machine's answer from punishment to gift.** sc34–35 land a **suspension** (निलंबन) — a punishment. The spine wants the kindest-looking knife: a forced **early-retirement dressed as a kindness** ("two years off, sahib, you've earned it"). Rana's own line in sc34 ("थका हुआ आदमी है… थोड़ा आराम दिला दो, घर पे") already points there — Mishra's delivery (sc35) is what needs reworking.
7. **Line-level / texture fixes.** Mute the **spoken Chambal legend** (sc14 — the "post-Heisenberg Walter" tell; build it gradually, don't state it). Weave in the **failing 58-yr-old body** (the knee, the offered painkillers he refuses because of the amal). Fix the **Govind "corpse in a uniform" line** (sc32 — the un-idiomatic Hindi). Give the autopsy the **load-shedding / phone-torch / flies** (sc22).
