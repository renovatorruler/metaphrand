# अमल — TITLE SEQUENCE (locked treatment)

*Plays over the main titles, every episode. Music = the **अमल instrumental title theme** (ElevenLabs,
`force_instrumental`), NOT the Jhujhar song (that's the saka leitmotif — cold open + ending). Theme of
the sequence = the **moral economy**: the scale (तौल) weighs everything human, and the patta outweighs
the child. No plot, no spoilers — symbol only.*

## The master image
The **brass weighment scale (तौल)**. The sequence returns to it again and again; the show's argument is
made by what is on the pans, escalating from the crop to the human.

## The beats (cut to the instrumental's build)
1. **Black.** A single brass weight drops onto a pan — hard metallic clack on the first beat.
2. **The belt, cold dawn.** White-and-purple poppy fields under grey mist; black soil cracked open. Vast, silent.
3. **The cut (macro).** A blade scores a green pod; white latex beads and bleeds. Beauty and a wound in one frame.
4. **Scale — opium.** Dark gum on one pan, brass weights on the other; weathered hands adjusting.
5. **Scale — cash.** Same scale, a banded brick of notes on the pan.
6. **Scale — the human.** A girl's red bridal bangles and veil on one pan, a stamped government **patta** on
   the other; the patta side sinks, the bangles rise. *The spine, made visual. Symbolic — no character.*
7. **Hands.** Fast montage: scoring the pod, stamping the patta, sliding notes under a blotter, a thumbprint.
8. **Rot under the beauty.** The field again, but a dry cracked well, dust lifting, a kite circling.
9. **Half-seen faces.** Partial, anonymous — a veiled woman's eyes, an old man's, a khaki shoulder. Never fully shown.
10. **Title.** The scale tips over, weights spilling; **अमल** forms (Pillow overlay — text banned in the
    gen). The theme swells and cuts to black.

## Look
Desaturated, dusty — black soil, khaki, grey dawn — with two accents only: the poppy's white-purple, and
the gold of brass and cash. Macro detail against the vast field. Slow, deliberate, prestige-noir
(Sacred Games / Paatal Lok register).

## Production
9 textless symbolic frames (Gemini, text-ban + visual QC) + the title overlay (Pillow); assembled to the
instrumental. Reused as the standing open for every episode.
