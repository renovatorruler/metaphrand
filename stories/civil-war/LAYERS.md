# INDIVISIBLE — the layer cards (PaRDeS, written as contracts)

**Form (user law, 2026-06-10):** no separate invariants field — the layers themselves are the
contract. **Peshat** is the plain sense of the page, which includes its silences: it is written in
two registers, *shows* and *withholds*, and the withheld is as binding as the shown. **Remez**
states the hint AND the condition that keeps it felt-not-stated. **Derash** is the sermon the
chapter aims; an added beat that serves a different sermon is wrong even if it's good. **Sod** is
off-page by definition — voicing it anywhere in the chapter is the violation.

**The gate (docs/05 step 0):** before any edit touches a chapter, read its card; the edit must
keep every *shows*, add nothing to a *withholds*, keep the Remez unstated, serve the Derash, and
leave the Sod unvoiced. Changing a card is a design decision made out loud, never an edit's side
effect. New chapters get their card before drafting.

**The book's Sod (no page, any part):** you can't flee America by wagon train; the wagon train is
America. PENDING is not a gap in the system — it is the system meeting its own future.

## 1 — The estate (the frame)
- **Peshat — shows:** a Black family in period dress at a lone farmhouse; the wife's window opens
  before the husband turns; four armed riders, dark as he is; the parley in plantation forms; the
  laminated recovery paper and the forty-dollars-gold bounty; the muscle's glance that skips the
  talker to the quiet old man; "Mount up"; the young one rising at the sight of the silver-cantled
  saddle; the wife's shot taking him mid-sentence; the fight; two dead, two out, the old man
  untouched and first to move; the marriage beat ("I had them leaving." / "You had them turning…
  Look again."); the hour-maybe-two clock; "Pack what you can carry. Ten minutes."
  **— withholds:** the hidden man, entirely — never seen, heard, named, or confirmed; no cellar,
  no voice, no fever, no family acknowledgment of him. The reader holds only the riders'
  description, the bay + saddle, good linen boiling on a Tuesday, Nia's unanswered WHOSE, and
  "Ten minutes was not going to be enough." The freight problem is implied, never stated. His
  identity and the fever-counting clue are held for the Part Two reveal.
- **Remez:** the slave patrol returned — and the hunters are his own. Carried by dress, dialect,
  the courtesy forms, the bounty paper; kept felt-not-stated by the slur living ONLY in the
  riders' mouths and the period illusion holding for the whole chapter — one quiet crack
  permitted, the laminate, filed odd and unexplained.
- **Derash:** the oldest sin doesn't stay buried; a family is measured by who it shelters when it
  comes back. Every beat must serve this sermon — Della firing first serves it (the household is
  the shelter); a domestic scene with the fugitive would not.
- **Sod:** it isn't the past — it's the future. Detonates only via ch.2's marker.
