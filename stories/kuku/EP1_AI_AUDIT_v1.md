## H0 — FRAGMENT DRAMA in Hindi dialogue (AUTHOR OVERRIDE — the #1 finding)
*Added after author review: the audit's original verdict of "largely clean" was WRONG.* The
staccato one-thought-per-fragment delivery («काला कुत्ता. मैंने बनाया.» / «मेरा कुत्ता, काला
वाला. ... मेरे लिए.») is (a) the megalist's Tier-3.6 fragment-drama tell reproduced in Hindi and
(b) actively harmful pedagogy — the kids absorb syntax from the show, so chopped Hindi teaches
chopped Hindi. LAW (BIBLE 2a): every line a complete grammatical sentence, short but whole.
Model correction (the author's): «पापा, पापा, देखो वो काला कुत्ता। आप एक कुत्ता मेरे लिए घर ले
आओ ना।» Related LAW (2b): children say आप to elders, never तुम/तू («तुम एक कुत्ता ले आओ» to
Papa was a register error). Fixed via Kuku_ReliftProper.res (all 7 scenes) same day.

Manual audit of all 7 receipted scenes against `docs/10-AI_MARKERS_MEGALIST.md`. The mechanical
