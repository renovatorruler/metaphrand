@@SCENE@@
ACTION: The sun comes up over the green valley. Dadi Maya, an old dragon with soft grey scales, sets a small clay lamp on a flat rock and waves Kuku over.
DADI MAYA: कुकु, आ जा।
ACTION: Kuku, a round little dragon barely knee-high, runs up to the lamp. Cheeku, a skinny fast dragonling, tumbles into the grass behind him.
KUKU: दादी, आग!
DADI MAYA: हाँ, आग। दीया जला दे।
ACTION: Kuku puffs out his cheeks and blows hard at the lamp. Only a thin curl of grey smoke drifts out.
KUKU: फिर से।
ACTION: He blows again, and again just a little cough of smoke comes out. The lamp stays dark and cold.
CHEEKU: कुकु! धुआँ! धुआँ!
ACTION: Cheeku giggles and rolls over in the grass, kicking his short legs in the air.
KUKU: मेरी आग नहीं आती।
ACTION: Kuku sits down on the rock and looks at his own two feet.
KUKU: कुकु आग नहीं।
DADI MAYA: कुकु, इधर देख।
DADI MAYA: आग रहने दे।
DADI MAYA: तुझे क्या अच्छा लगता है?
KUKU: दोस्त। चीकू। माँ।
DADI MAYA: दोस्त? बहुत अच्छा।
DADI MAYA: अब वही सोच, कुकु।
DADI MAYA: फिर से कोशिश कर।
ACTION: Dadi Maya leans her big head down close to Kuku, so Cheeku out in the grass can't hear.
DADI MAYA (WHISPER): तू कर सकता है, कुकु।
ACTION: Kuku looks over at Cheeku. He shuts his eyes and pulls in one big, slow breath that puffs his whole round belly out.
KUKU: कुकु... फिर से।
ACTION: He breathes out toward the sky. No fire comes. A glowing golden shape floats up out of his mouth and hangs in the air over the rock.
CHEEKU: देखो! देखो!
ACTION: Cheeku scrambles up and points at the shape in the sky.
KUKU: मेरा? मेरा है?
DADI MAYA: हाँ कुकु, तेरा है।
DADI MAYA: ये अक्षर है। ये "क" है।
KUKU: क?
DADI MAYA: क। कुकु का क।
ACTION: The glowing क drifts down low over the little clay lamp and fills the whole rock with warm golden light.
KUKU: देखो! क! मेरा क!
CHEEKU: फिर से कुकु! फिर से!
DADI MAYA: शाबाश कुकु।
KUKU: कुकु का क!
