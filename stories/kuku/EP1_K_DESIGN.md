# Episode 1 — क (Kuku aur Akshar) — Design

*The dramatized scenes (segments 1, 2, 4) are the SEED for the `studio/` engine — see `studio/src/Kuku_WriteEp1.res`. The engine writes the prose; this doc is structure only. Segments 3 & 5 (letter lesson, goodbye) are the pedagogical template below.*

**Letter:** क · **Words:** कुत्ता (dog) · कान (ear) · केला (banana) · **Hero-hook:** कुकु ने भी क से! (Kuku starts with क too!)

## The tiny arc (the story that earns the letter)
The littlest dragon can't light दादी माया's morning lamp — no fire comes. He's sad. Dadi tells him to try again and breathe what he *loves*. He does — and out floats not fire but a glowing **क**. His "failure" is a gift; क is even in his own name. The valley celebrates "कुकु का क."

## Segment map

### 1 — शुरू / Story A (ENGINE)
Morning in Akshar Ghaati. Kuku wants to light Dadi's दीया like the big dragons do. He puffs — only smoke. चीकू giggles. Kuku deflates: he can't make fire.

### 2 — The breath (ENGINE)
Dadi: don't chase fire — breathe what you love. Kuku thinks of his friends, takes a huge breath — a glowing **क** rises into the sky. Everyone gasps: *it's an अक्षर!*

### 2.5 — The टोपी (hat) beat (NEW — the disambiguation engine; see BIBLE Part VI.5)
Kuku's breath first makes a **hatless shape** — a circle-on-a-stick that floats, not-quite-a-letter
(it looks like Ф, the letter Furia's name starts with). Beat of "hmm, что?" Then the **टोपी snaps
on with a pop** — now it is क.
- Dadi: «टोपी बिना, ये अक्षर नहीं… टोपी लगी — अब ये है, क!»
- Optional say-it-to-Furia line: «Ф डंडे पे गेंद है। कुकु का क टोपी पहनता है।» (Ф is a ball on a
  stick; Kuku's क wears a hat.)
- Reinforced in the video: the typographic क overlay (Kohinoor) shows the hat clearly.

### 3 — आज का अक्षर / Letter Lesson (TEMPLATE — Dadi to camera)
- **Name:** «आज का अक्षर है — क!» *(EN gloss: "Today's letter is — K!")*
- **Sound:** «क… क… क!» (air-trace the shape together)
- **Call-and-response:** «बोलो मेरे साथ — क!» → (kids) «क!» → «शाबाश! फिर से — क!»
- **Hero-hook:** «कुकु! 'कुकु' में भी क है! क-ुकु!»
- **Three picture-words** (Hindi-first, क emphasized, one-word EN gloss, show the picture):
  1. **कु**त्ता 🐶 *(dog)* — «क से कुत्ता!»
  2. **का**न 👂 *(ear)* — «क से कान!» (point to your own ear)
  3. **के**ला 🍌 *(banana)* — «क से केला!»
- **Letter song / chant** (spoken-sung, highly repetitive — DRAFT, native check pending):
  > क से कुत्ता, क से कान,
  > क से केला — बड़ा मज़ेदार!
  > क… क… क — बोलो क!
  > क… क… क — कुकु का क!

### 4 — कहानी वापस / Story B (ENGINE)
The glowing क lights the room like a little lamp — Kuku's letter *is* his light. चीकू begs "फिर से! फिर से!" Kuku breathes क again, beaming. Everyone: «कुकु का क!»

### 5 — अलविदा / Recap + goodbye (TEMPLATE — Dadi to camera)
- «आज हमने सीखा — क!» *(EN gloss: "Today we learned — K!")*
- «क से?» → (kids) «कुत्ता! कान! केला!»
- «कल फिर मिलेंगे। अलविदा!» + goodbye wave/sing-off.
