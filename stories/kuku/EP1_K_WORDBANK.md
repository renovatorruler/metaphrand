# Episode 1 — क — Word Bank (picturable, preschool)

The palette the script pulls from. Rule: concrete, showable on screen, in a 3–5-year-old's world.
The letter's words must be **woven into the story as props and jokes**, never a flashcard list.
⭐ = cleanest "ka" sound and/or strongest for the "Kuku wants a dog" plot.

## The dog's world is already क
A **काला कुत्ता** (black dog) with big **कान** (ears) that loves to **कूदना** (jump),
eats from a **कटोरा** (bowl), sleeps on a **कंबल** (blanket), rolls in the **कीचड़** (mud) —
and **कुत्ता केला नहीं खाता** (a dog doesn't eat a banana).
~7 क-words living naturally inside one story about wanting a dog.
