### "Kuku and the Peacock Chick"

**Model:** same Super Kitties engine as Ep1 — real stakes, teamwork of gifts, power-up chant,
no villain. All series laws bind: Sesame-forward letter placement, टोपी beat, proper-Hindi 2a/2b
(complete sentences; आप to elders), «मैं पहले!», «हिंदी में, वैस्पर!», Vesper-asleep button,
filler-take + reference-sheet production laws.

**Letter:** म · **Anchor:** दादी **मा**या + **माँ** · **Words lived through:** माँ · मेला (fair) ·
मिठाई (sweets) · मोर (peacock) · मछली (fish) · मदद (help) · माया. (LETTER_MAP's माँ/मछली/मोर all in-plot.)

**Logline:** At the valley मेला, a peacock chick loses its माँ in the crowd — and the one thing
that can carry it home across the stream is the letter of the day: म, whose loop cradles like a
mother's arms.

**Sod (never on the page):** everyone is someone's child; a mother's love is the first address
you ever learn. (The chick has a माँ. Papa has a माँ — she's दादी. माशा far away has a माँ.
Nobody says the theme; it lives in the reunions.)

## Continuity from Ep1
कालू is Kuku's dog now and comes along (his nose becomes a team gift). Papa's arc is settled —
he appears warm, no conflict. The golden letter prop, Furia's किताब, and the corner-bug letter

## The letter beat evolves (Ep2's twist on the ritual)
- **Furia's Ф gag passes the torch to Vesper:** he sees the golden म and drifts into English —
  "that's M!" → «हिंदी में, वैस्पर!» → Dadi: English M **doesn't wear a hat** — «म टोपी पहनता
  है, M नहीं.» (The टोपी law generalizing beyond Furia's Ф — the rule, not the exception.)
- **Hero-hook:** «'माया' में म है!» — it's Dadi's own letter. And the deeper hook lands in s5:
  दादी माया is पापा की माँ. (Preschool mind = blown, and it's pure म.)
- **Power-up:** «साँस, टोपी, अक्षर!» unchanged (series constant). The म's **loop becomes a
  cradle-boat** — the letter literally holds the chick the way a माँ holds a baby.

## Running gag (Ep2's «कुत्ता केला नहीं खाता»)
The chick imprints on कालू and follows him everywhere, cheeping. Everyone, in chorus:
**«कालू माँ नहीं है!»** (Kalu is not a mom!) — कालू increasingly bewildered. Pays off when the
real माँ appears and the chick finally lets go of the poor dog.

## Beat sheet (7 scenes, Ep1's proven shape)
- **s0 Teaser — the letter.** A चिट्ठी arrives — from **माशा**! A postcard: she went to a big
  मेला in her city. The kids erupt: OUR मेला is today! कालू barks, Cheeku bangs his spoon,
  everyone runs. (Dragonmail motif; माशा reference only, per plan.)
- **s1 आज का अक्षर.** Dadi reveals the golden **म**. Vesper's "that's M!" → the hat rule.
  «बोलो मेरे साथ — म!» Hero-hook: «'माया' में म है!» Corner-bug parks. Prime: «मेले में म
  ढूँढो!» Furia vows to find the most.
- **s2 The मेला.** Wonder tour: Dadi's **मिठाई** stall, the **मछली** pond, and the **मोर**
  family parading — the chicks trailing their माँ. म-words ding. Furia races everything;
  Vesper drifts after balloons; the chick first mistakes कालू's tail for its mother («कालू
  माँ नहीं है!» #1).
- **s3 Lost.** Drums, crowd, noise — and suddenly a tiny **मोर का बच्चा** alone, crying. Furia

  nowhere. Kuku: «हमें मदद करनी है.» कालू sniffs the ground — he can track! The chick glues

- **s4 The search + power-up.** कालू tracks to the stream's edge — scent ends at the water.
  **Vesper**, staring at "clouds," goes still: «वो बादल नहीं... वो मोरनी है!» — the माँ, far

- **s5 The heart.** Back at the मिठाई stall. Kuku, watching the peacock family: «दादी, क्या

  up, kisses Dadi's head: «हाँ, ये मेरी माँ हैं.» मिठाई all around; कालू gets one; the chick's
  family parades past in thanks. («कालू माँ नहीं है!» #3 — the chick waves goodbye to कालू.)
- **s6 Recap + goodnight.** Star-lit rock: «म से?» → «माँ! मेला! मिठाई! मोर! मछली! मदद!

  किताब — «मैं पहले!» Pan: **Vesper asleep in Dadi's empty मिठाई basket**, कालू curled beside
  it. «शुभ रात्रि, वैस्पर.»
