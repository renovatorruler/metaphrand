# कुकु और काला कुत्ता — Episode 1: क — Plot v1
### "Kuku and the Black Dog"

**Model:** Super Kitties tempo — real stakes, a power-up beat, humor, teamwork, heart; the
lesson baked into the action, NOT a flashcard break. Audience recalibrated: **not-so-young**
(~4–7, Super Kitties viewers). Story sophistication HIGH; the teachable Hindi stays clear and
repetition-friendly (the action carries the sophistication, the vocabulary stays learnable).

**Logline:** Kuku wants a dog more than anything, but Papa says no — until a scared little black
puppy tumbles into real danger, and the only thing that can save it is the one "useless" thing
Kuku can do: breathe a letter.

**Sod (never on the page):** the "weird" thing about Kuku — letters instead of fire — is exactly
what saves the day. Difference = gift.

## The power-up (Super-Kitties signature)
Chant: **«साँस… टोपी… अक्षर!»** (Breath… Hat… Letter!) — teaches the टोपी/hat rule every time
(see BIBLE Part VI.5, the क-vs-Cyrillic-Ф disambiguation).

## The क-words we LIVE THROUGH (props/jokes, not flashcards)
कुत्ता (dog) · कालू (the dog's name) · काला (black) · कान (ears) · कूदना (jump) · कीचड़ (mud) ·
कुआँ (well) · कटोरा (bowl) · कंबल (blanket) · केला (banana — running gag).

## Cast this episode — THE TRIO (locked 2026-07-21)
**कुकु** (hero, letter-breath) · **फ़ूरिया / Furia** (sister — first at everything, kind-competitive,
dreams of a red कार, writes letters in her किताब; the Ф gag is hers) · **वैस्पर / Vesper**
(her younger brother — dreamy, called 4–5 times before answering, drifts into English and is
reminded «हिंदी में, वैस्पर!», falls asleep anywhere; hears कालू first) · **पापा** (Kuku's dad,
kind-but-firm no) · **दादी माया** (host) · **कालू** (the black puppy) · **चीकू** (now the toddler
Furia & Vesper look after). Furia+Vesper = siblings, Kuku's best friends next door (NOT his
siblings — the dog conflict stays between Kuku and HIS Papa).

Characters mirror the author's real kids — see VOICE_CARDS. Their letters get spotlight
episodes: **फ (Furia)** and **व (Vesper)**. NOTE for Ep2 (म): a REFERENCE to Anna & Masha only
(माशा starts with म — e.g. a letter from माशा arrives); guest appearance in a later episode.

## Running gag
Everyone keeps offering the dog a banana → «कुत्ता केला नहीं खाता!» (a dog doesn't eat a banana).
Pays off as the final button.

## Letter placement — SESAME-FORWARD (LOCKED)
The written क is introduced UP FRONT and stays on screen all episode as a "letter-of-the-day"
corner bug. Four touches:
- **Top:** Dadi shows the written क + टोपी + sound, and Furia's Ф gag → hat clarification, right away.
- **Throughout:** each क-word DINGS/GLOWS as it appears (kids already know the target → treasure hunt).
- **Climax:** the letter they learned becomes Kuku's power (the टोपी-snap power-up).
- **Recap:** consolidate.

## Beat sheet (Sesame-forward)
0. **Teaser (~15s) — The Want.** Kuku plays "dog": paper कान strapped on, भौं-भौं, begging for a
   stick. Runs to पापा with a crayon drawing of his dream **काला कुत्ता**. Papa: «नहीं, कुकु।
   कुत्ता बहुत काम है। तुम अभी छोटे हो।» Kuku deflates. Fast hook + want + wall.
1. **आज का अक्षर — the intro (UP FRONT).** Dadi to camera: «आज का अक्षर है — क!» Shows the big
   written **क with its टोपी**; sound «क… क… क». **Furia races in FIRST** («मैं पहले!») to answer —
   then freezes: «अरे, ये तो मेरा Ф है!» → «नहीं — देखो, क टोपी पहनता है!» (the disambiguation, up
   front, and it's HER bit). A small **क** parks in the corner for the rest of the episode. Dadi
   primes the hunt: «कान खोलो — आज क ढूँढो!» Furia vows to find the most क-words. (Vesper, asked
   what the letter is, answers a beat late: «…hmm? — क!»)
2. **Act 1 — The Puppy.** By the **कुआँ**: **Vesper**, lost in the clouds while everyone talks,
   is the only one who hears a tiny whimper. It takes three calls of «वैस्पर!» before he points:
   a muddy, scared **काला कुत्ता**, कान drooping, stuck in the **कीचड़**. **Furia gets there
   first** — then goes instantly gentle, mothering it while Kuku coaxes it free. Kuku names it
   **कालू**. Vesper: "He's so cute—" → «हिंदी में, वैस्पर!» → «…कितना प्यारा!» Gag: Kuku offers a
   **केला** → «कुत्ता केला नहीं खाता!» (क-words dinging: कुआँ, कीचड़, काला, कुत्ता, कान.)
3. **Act 2 — Hiding & Chaos.** The trio hides कालू from Papa. Fast montage: कालू **कूदना**
   everywhere, rolling in **कीचड़**, knocking over Dadi's **कटोरा**s — and nearly flattening
   toddler **चीकू** (Furia scoops the baby clear: caretaking beat). Kuku builds a **कंबल** bed;
   Furia fetches the **कटोरा**; Vesper is assigned lookout… and drifts off watching a butterfly —
   which is exactly why Papa nearly walks in. Wall rises: the mess mounts, Kuku wobbles — maybe
   a dog IS too much काम.
4. **Act 3 — Danger + the Power-Up.** कालू chases a **कबूतर** toward the **कुआँ**, slips over the
   edge, clings to the rim. **Furia charges first** — too small, comedic near-miss (Vesper catches
   her tail). **Vesper**, calm and dreamy, sees what no one else does: «ऊपर से… हुक जैसा…» (it
   needs a hook from above). Kuku, terrified (echo of the fire), powers up: «साँस… टोपी… अक्षर!»
   — hatless ball first («अभी नहीं!»), **टोपी SNAPS on → «अब — क!»** — **the क they learned at
   the top becomes the hook** Vesper imagined, and it lifts कालू to safety. Three kids, three
   gifts, one rescue.
5. **Resolution — The Earn.** Papa saw it all — bravery, care, that letter. «कालू तुम्हारा है,
   कुकु।» कालू eats from the **कटोरा**; someone offers a **केला** → «कुत्ता केला नहीं खाता!» —
   biggest laugh.
6. **Recap + button (integrated).** Montage of the क-things we just lived
   (कुत्ता–कालू–काला–कान–कूदना–कीचड़–कुआँ–कटोरा–कंबल–केला), each flashing with its क + टोपी.
   «क से?» → «कुत्ता!» Rule: «हिंदी का हर अक्षर टोपी पहनता है!» **Button, all three signatures
   stacked:** Furia proudly shows she wrote क in her **किताब** — first, obviously → pan over:
   **Vesper is already asleep on the कंबल, curled around कालू.** Dadi, soft: «शुभ रात्रि,
   वैस्पर।» Out.
