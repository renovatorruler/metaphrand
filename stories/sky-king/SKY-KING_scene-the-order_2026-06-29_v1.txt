INT. INCIDENT COMMAND POST — NIGHT

Folding tables. Radios. The city out the window, far down.

HOLT stands over a chart, sleeves rolled. REYES comes in still in his
suit, county lanyard, woken from somewhere.

REYES
They said a plane.

HOLT
Baggage handler. Took a turboprop off the south apron.

REYES
And you want a prosecutor.

HOLT
I want him on the ground.

Reyes looks at the radio. At the dark beyond the glass — two faint
shapes holding off the wing lights of the third.

REYES
What am I telling him.

HOLT
Whatever puts him down.

A beat. Reyes waits for more. Holt goes back to the chart.

HOLT (CONT'D)
You're better with people than I am.

He doesn't look up. Reyes takes the headset.

INTERCUT — THE COCKPIT

BIRDY, small in the seat. The yoke loose in his hands. BISHOP's voice
steady in his ear, mid-sentence.

BISHOP (V.O.)
— got somebody here wants to say hello. That alright.

BIRDY
Sure.

REYES (V.O.)
Birdy. My name's Reyes. I work for the county.

Birdy watches the lights of a jet slide closer, then ease back.

BIRDY
They woke a lawyer up for me.                <!-- fix: was the echo "County." -->

REYES (V.O.)
I'm not here to make this bigger than it is. You come on in, we sit
down, we work out what this really is. It won't be the thing you're
picturing.

Birdy is quiet. The engines drone.

BIRDY
You can do that.

REYES (V.O.)
I can sit at the table. That's a start, isn't it.

A long beat. Birdy's eyes go to the city below, all of it lit.

BIRDY
It's a nice view from here.

He doesn't say no. He doesn't say yes. The jet holds on his wing.

BISHOP (V.O.)
Still got you, Birdy.

BIRDY
Yeah.

He keeps flying.
