## Spine
He's too nice and too weak — a gifted coward. One day, freed by having nothing
left to lose, he stops being a coward: he takes a plane. Up there the world sees
his gift and is AWED, and the recognition makes him want to live. He lives — and
the sentence is that he must keep living brave. Mirror: we're all the talented
coward. NO VILLAIN — the system is honest (rewards nerve, not talent); the
authorities are consequences after he acts, never causes.
