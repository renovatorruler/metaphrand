@@SCENE@@
ACTION: A folding table scrapes across the linoleum as two guys in windbreakers carry it past and set it down against the wall.
ACTION: MERCER, fifties, jacket off and sleeves rolled, a phone pressed to his ear, walks the length of the room stepping over a cable somebody is taping to the floor.
MERCER: Yeah, I've got you. Put the second one on the bridge line and keep it open, I don't want to be dialing anybody twice tonight.
ACTION: A wall screen wakes up blue, then black, then a slow green track appears looping out over the water, and a tech leans in and thumbs two small tags onto it that ride along behind the loop.
ACTION: MERCER lowers the phone from his ear and points at a woman seating agencies at the tables.
MERCER: Where are we on the human side. I've got a room and I've got a picture and I don't have anybody to work the guy.
ACTION: The woman shakes her head and keeps plugging in a monitor.
ACTION: REYES comes through the door, forties, dressed and quick, a bag over one shoulder, still pulling her badge around on its lanyard, looking for whoever's in charge.
REYES: They pulled me out of my kitchen. Somebody tell me what I've got.
ACTION: MERCER crosses to her and puts the phone facedown against his chest for a second.
MERCER: You're the negotiator. Good. Okay. There's an airplane up.
ACTION: MERCER turns and tips his head at the screen with the looping green track.
MERCER: One of the airline's turboprops off Sea-Tac. Empty, no passengers, and there's one guy flying it. That's what we've got from the tower and from Kemp's call.
REYES: One guy. And he's, what — crew? A pilot?
MERCER: He's not on anybody's roster. He told the tower it isn't his, the airplane. That's the whole thing we know about him.
ACTION: A phone starts ringing on one of the new tables and nobody gets to it and it keeps going.
REYES: Okay. Anybody else up there with him, in the cabin, anybody hurt that we know of.
MERCER: No. Just him.
ACTION: MERCER lifts the phone back to his ear, says "hold on," and lowers it again.
MERCER: And there's two fighters on him. Those are the two tags you see sitting on the track. That's already up, that came up with the military side, that's not us.
ACTION: REYES looks at the two tags riding the green loop and sets her bag down against a table leg.
REYES: Right. So before I get near him I need to know what I'm authorized to offer him. What can I actually put on the table.
ACTION: MERCER glances at a printer that's grinding out a page and doesn't answer for a second.
MERCER: Whatever gets him down.
ACTION: REYES stands there a beat.
REYES: I hear you, but I mean the real answer, Mercer. If I say a thing to this man, is there paper behind it. Is there somebody I can name who's good for it.
ACTION: MERCER's phone lights again in his hand and he silences it against his leg.
MERCER: There's you. You get him on the ground.
ACTION: MERCER holds her look, then turns toward the tables filling with badges.
REYES: So there's nothing to point to. It's me saying it, and then it's the same job over again.
MERCER: That's what I've got tonight.
ACTION: Neither of them says anything, and around them a fourth screen wakes up and two more agents come in with coffee and set their go-bags on the floor.
ACTION: MERCER steps back in and lowers his voice under the room.
MERCER (WHISPER): I can't define it for you. You know I can't. The second I define it it's mine, and then it's slow, and we don't have slow.
ACTION: REYES nods, once, and doesn't push it.
REYES: Okay. Then give me a place to start, because right now I've got nobody. No name, no file, nothing, I'm talking to a hole.
MERCER: Two things, in order. Find out who he is first. Then you get him down.
ACTION: MERCER points across the room at a door where a duty officer is walking two men in high-visibility vests through security.
MERCER: Airline's bringing their people in over there. There's a ramp boss and somebody who saw him. Start with them, get me a name.
ACTION: A RADIO on the tech's desk crackles and a flat voice comes through it.
CONTROLLER (RADIO): Command, we're still holding him over the water, he's just making the turn again, no change.
ACTION: MERCER is already walking, the phone coming back up to his ear.
MERCER: Copy, keep him talking to somebody.
ACTION: REYES doesn't move. She stands with her bag at her feet and looks up at the screen, at the slow green track making its loop and the two fast tags riding it, while behind her the phones ring and the tables keep filling.
