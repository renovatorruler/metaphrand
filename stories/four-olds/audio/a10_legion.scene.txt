ACTION: A wood stove ticks in the corner of the Legion back room.
ACTION: Mack Boone, fifty-five, the freight hauler who got them the contract, sets a thin folder on the card table and slides a sheet halfway out.
ACTION: Cricket, Gunny, Stitch, and Dutch sit around the table with their coffee going cold.
MACK: Read that before I say a word. Then you can throw me out if you want.
ACTION: Mack pushes the top sheet into the middle of the table.
MACK: (reading) Lunar Heritage Neutralization Accord, artifact retrieval. Six containment units, class-A archival. Outbound configuration, empty.
ACTION: Dutch picks the sheet up and reads the whole of it through twice before he sets it back down.
DUTCH: Six boxes. They go up empty.
MACK: And they come back down with the flags in them.
ACTION: Nobody says anything, and the stove ticks.
ACTION: Mack turns the sheet over to a printed email and reads it off with no color in his voice.
MACK: (reading) Containment units are non-crew hardware. No certification pathway required. Recommend sourcing retired fabrication and aerospace personnel at heritage-labor rates.
ACTION: He lays the page down where they can all see it.
MACK: Heritage-labor rates. That's you, gentlemen. That's this whole room.
ACTION: Stitch turns his cup a quarter turn on the table.
STITCH: Cheap old men. To box up the—
ACTION: He lets it go, and not one man at the table takes it up for him.
ACTION: Dutch already has the spec page out of his own packet and runs a thumb down the margin.
DUTCH: This is my standard. The '98 archival spec. Word for word.
MACK: Nobody's improved it.
ACTION: Gunny stands and squares his chair back against the wall, and he stays on his feet.
GUNNY: Somebody's going to fold those colors into them, on live television, with the whole world grading the job. I'd sooner it be men who know how a flag is handled than whoever else answers that email. We're the whoever-else, boys.
ACTION: A short, hard laugh goes around the table and dies out fast.
ACTION: Mack deals a stapled packet to each man like he's dealing cards.
ACTION: Papers riffle around the table, and only Dutch turns through the whole of his.
ACTION: Mack taps the last page with one finger.
MACK: No phones in the shop, no pictures. And whatever gets built in there, it stays inside those walls.
ACTION: Stitch signs his without turning past the first page.
STITCH: Confidential flag boxes.
MACK: That's the job.
ACTION: Gunny signs, and Dutch signs under him, slow, down at the bottom of the last page.
ACTION: Cricket slides his own packet back to the middle with no mark on it, puts his cap on, and gets up from the table.
ACTION: The back door bangs shut behind him.
ACTION: Out in the lot his boots grind across the gravel and go quiet.
ACTION: Nobody moves, and Gunny pours a cup off the percolator he won't drink.
ACTION: The gravel crunches again, the door opens, and Cricket comes back in with his cap still on.
ACTION: He takes his packet up off the table, turns to the spec page, and reads it through standing where he is.
ACTION: He picks up the pen and signs last, at the bottom, under Dutch.
