ACTION: Inside the Frontier shop, a television plays on a shelf in the corner, up over a bin of fittings, where two morning-show hosts sit on a bright couch. Joss stands under it with the remote in his hand, twenty-six, in Frontier dock coveralls with grease to the elbow.

ACTION: Stitch leans on the vise and watches the little television, seventy-four, a Texan, a shop rag hung over one shoulder.
