# THE HEIR — beat sheet (the descent → 40 feature scenes)

~120 pages, ~3pp/scene avg (funeral, the doorway sequence, the square run long). Each line:
`N. SLUG — POV — want / turn`. `[TURN n]` = the deepening descent turns. `[valley]` = a modulation
beat (release from the action, never from the dread). The Sod (he becomes his father; *demir* = iron;
the Jungian shadow) is never spoken — it's the thing every scene is built to dramatize.

## KI — the relief (Act 1a)
1. INT. RESIDENCE, ASLAN'S ROOM - NIGHT — Nadir — his father dies; the relief he can't show; Bayar already at the door, "Mr. President." [open]
2. EXT. STATE FUNERAL, ASTAN - DAY — Nadir — the Lion buried under his own cult; Nadir's careful eulogy; at the open grave, the silent vow: nothing like him.
3. INT. RESIDENCE, NADIR & LENA - NIGHT — Lena — [valley] the marriage, the dream; "we can actually do it now."
4. INT. CABINET ROOM - DAY — Nadir — meets the inherited old guard and Bayar; announces he's bringing Yusuf in; the room cools.
5. INT. AIRPORT, ARRIVALS - DAY — Yusuf — [valley] Yusuf lands from London; the two friends; "the good chapter."

## SHŌ — the windows, read as weakness (Act 1b–2A)
6. INT. THE OFFICE (SECURITY HQ) - DAY — Nadir — tours the Office, orders files opened; Bayar courteously gives him rope.
7. EXT. PRISON GATES - DAY — Nadir — political prisoners walk free; cameras; "the reformer prince." The high point.
8. INT. RESIDENCE / TV - NIGHT — Lena — [valley] a paper prints a criticism and no one is arrested; Lena's quiet joy.
9. INT. CABINET ROOM - DAY — Yusuf — Yusuf's reform program; the old ministers stonewall; Bayar watches.
10. INT. SITUATION ROOM - DAY — Nadir — the eastern oil province stops remitting; the first crack.
11. INT. SECURE LINE - NIGHT — Nadir — the northern envoy: troops "for exercises." The neighbor tests him.
12. EXT. ASTAN SQUARE - DAY — Nadir — a protest forms; demand, not thanks; it grows.
13. INT. THE OFFICE - NIGHT — Bayar — lays out the situation and two options, the iron and the soft. The vise opens.
14. INT. RESIDENCE - NIGHT — Lena — she warns; he insists there's another way. [Act 1 break: the east falls without force.]

## TEN — the turns (Act 2)
15. INT. WAR ROOM - DAY — Nadir — lose the east, lose the country; the generals push for force.
16. INT. NADIR'S OFFICE - NIGHT — Nadir — Yusuf argues against; Nadir signs the order. "Not his kind of order." [TURN 1]
17. INT. SITUATION / FOOTAGE - DAY — Nadir — the east retaken; it works; the stain.
18. INT. RESIDENCE - NIGHT — Lena — "you said just this once." The first fracture.
19. INT. THE OFFICE, ARCHIVE - DAY — Yusuf — dismantling the sealed archive, he finds Aslan's young boxes.
20. INT. ARCHIVE - NIGHT — Yusuf — reads the young Lion: a reformer, the same dream, almost the same words. He sees it.
21. INT. NADIR'S OFFICE - NIGHT — Nadir — Yusuf brings the father's young charter: "He was you." [THE RE-READ]
22. INT. FLASHBACK — YOUNG ASLAN, A SQUARE - DAY — the only flashback: young Aslan gives the same speech. [dad was him]
23. INT. CABINET ROOM - DAY — Nadir — the chaos the reforms let in escalates; the old guard demands order.
24. EXT. ASTAN - NIGHT — Nadir — a bombing, blamed on the opening; bodies; the country turns.
25. INT. THE OFFICE - DAY — Bayar — the scapegoat logic: the disorder needs a name, and the only one big enough is Yusuf's.
26. INT. NADIR'S OFFICE - NIGHT — Nadir — [valley, unbearable] the last good talk with Yusuf, who doesn't know.
27. INT. RESIDENCE - NIGHT — Lena — Nadir tells her what's being asked; "don't"; he's silent.
28. INT. CELL CORRIDOR - DAY — Nadir — he gives the nod; they take Yusuf; he stays at the desk. [TURN 2 — the doorway of no return]
29. INT. CELL - NIGHT — Yusuf — "Tell me you didn't know. Say it." Nadir doesn't.
30. INT. RESIDENCE - NIGHT — Lena — packing, deciding: "I married Nate. I keep waiting for him in the car."
31. EXT. ASTAN SQUARE - DAY — Nadir — the square swells to a million after Yusuf. The moment that ends regimes.

## TEN → KETSU — turn three, the becoming complete (Act 3)
32. INT. WAR ROOM - DAY — Nadir — clear it or fall; the order on the desk.
33. INT. NADIR'S OFFICE - NIGHT — Nadir — alone; his father's voice in him now. "Clear the square. I don't want the number." [TURN 3]
34. EXT. THE SQUARE - DAWN — Nadir — the aftermath of the clearing (violence off-page); it worked; the country holds.
35. INT. RESIDENCE - DAY — Nadir — Lena gone; the empty rooms; the door she'd watched.
36. INT. THE OFFICE - DAY — Bayar — "Your father would have done it before lunch." The apparatus accepts him.
37. INT. STATE TV / WEST DESK - DAY — Nadir — "Nadir the Bloody"; the West signs the oil deals anyway.

## KETSU — no exception
38. INT. RESIDENCE, ASLAN'S OLD ROOM - NIGHT — Nadir — alone where his father died; he understands the man now; the buried charter.
39. INT. OFFICE, RECEPTION - DAY — Nadir — a young idealistic aide, just home from a Western school, meets his eyes and looks away first; Nadir knows the look. [CYCLE RE-ARMS / no_exception]
40. INT. NADIR'S OFFICE - NIGHT — Nadir — the chair, the silence; he became his father exactly, awake the whole way, and there was never a door. FADE OUT.
