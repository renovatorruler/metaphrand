## Locked premise
*The heir.* **Nadir Demir**, gentle London-educated son of **Aslan Demir** ("the Lion"), iron founder of the
Caspian oil republic of **Karash**. At his father's grave he swears to be nothing like him; to keep the throne
he becomes everything he hated about him — and stays awake the whole way down. A **descent**: each "just this
once" deepens, there is no exception, the cycle re-arms. Jungian frame stays submerged (Sod); *demir* = iron,
never stated. Voice compass: **Elmore Leonard**. Engine: metaphrand (`stories/civil-war-novel-bible.md` is the
sister format; `docs/03-VOICE_GUIDE.md` is the law).
