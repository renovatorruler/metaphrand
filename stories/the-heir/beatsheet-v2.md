## ACT 1 — the refusal and the forcing (the part v1 was missing)
1. INT. LONDON - LAW CHAMBERS - DAY — Nadir the lawyer: warm, loose, articulate-but-hedging (his EARLY voice). The foreign call he won't answer — "a wrong number that's been wrong for fifteen years." [refusal established]
2. INT. LONDON - THE FLAT - NIGHT — Lena. The Lion is dying; "he asked for me." Nadir: I bury him and I come home, three days, we never say the name of that country in this flat again. Neither believes it. [refusal + dramatic irony]
3. INT. KARASH - ASLAN'S ROOM - NIGHT — Nadir arrives from the airport, still in his coat, not staying. The Lion dies. The relief, the "Okay," the monitor reflex. Bayar at the door: "Mr. President." (= the trap's first tooth, not a coronation he wants.) [keep, re-framed]
4. INT. THE OFFICE / A SECURE ROOM - NIGHT — THE DETONATION. The body isn't cold and **General Rustam** has moved — the eastern garrison, the airport road, the state TV. A coup, in the open. Bayar lays it out: the heir's legitimacy is the only thing that outranks Rustam's guns; take the chair tonight or Rustam takes the country, and takes Nadir's head, because an heir is the one thing a usurper can't allow to live. [forcing crisis + RIVAL + clock starts]
5. INT./EXT. THE PALACE - NIGHT — Nadir tries to refuse, tries to leave. The border's shut, his name's on Rustam's list, Lena can't be flown out. The trap closes. He meets Rustam (or hears him) — blunt, contemptuous, "the boy." [clock tightens; refusal collides with reality]
6. INT. WAR ROOM / BAYAR'S OFFICE - NIGHT — the apparatus is deciding which way to fall. Bayar shows Nadir the only move that stops Rustam: his father's move, cold and total. Nadir resists it. Turan (honest soldier) tells him the truth: there is no clean way to survive tonight. [the iron offered]
7. INT. THE PALACE - DAWN — [TURN 1 — first forced iron] Nadir does it: turns Rustam's own men, or has him taken in the dark, by a stroke so cold it surprises everyone, himself most. Rustam falls. Nadir holds the country by morning — and is appalled at how well the cold fit his hand. He chose to SPARE Rustam's faction (mercy; his refusal of his father's totality) — the seed of the betrayal to come. [he takes the throne he fled]
8. INT. RESIDENCE - DAY — aftermath. Lena: now we go home. Nadir: I can't — if I let go now it collapses, they kill us, the neighbor walks in. Just until it's stable. He'll rule as himself. Clean. ("Just this once" of staying.) [the lie that starts the descent]
