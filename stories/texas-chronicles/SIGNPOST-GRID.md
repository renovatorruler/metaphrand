# TEXAS CHRONICLES — The Signpost Grid

The whole series as one film, cut into independently-watchable shorts, mapped onto **James Scott
Bell's 14 signposts**. World authority = `stories/ancapistan/ARENA.md`. Series directives =
`NOTES.md`.

## The spine (one sentence)
A broke Union man inherits a stake in a Dallas insurance agency, comes to **cash out and run**, and
is turned — case by case — into an Ancapistan man who **chooses the place over the nation he was born
to.** The master hand carries every case; the heir (our Watson) carries the transformation.

## The through-line (connective tissue — PLACEHOLDER, defer to held cards)
The Union's reach follows the heir into the Free States and escalates beneath the standalone cases
until it forces a final choice of sides. **Flagged:** the real macro-engine may tie to Cainland's
hidden structure (author's card) — swap freely; the grid's standalone logic survives either way.

## Two design rules baked in
- **Each short = a complete case × one concept × one beat of the heir's turn.** Watch one, you get a
  whole story; watch all, you've seen the movie.
- **Concept complexity escalates with the drama.** Act 1 + early Act 2 teach ONE concept per short;
  late Act 2 + Act 3 combine them (audience now fluent). The teaching load front-loads; the
  full-system showdowns back-load.

Premise carried in every short on three layers (see NOTES.md): structural (the case is private-law),
the fixed cold-open signature, and the heir as walking orientation.

## ACT ONE — the ordinary world and the doorway in

**1 · DISTURBANCE — "No Service"**
Case: he crosses into the Free States to claim his late uncle's estate; his phone is a brick, and the
answer to "why" is Union jets over Dallas. He learns the uncle left him a *stake in an insurance
agency*, not just land. · Concept: the information regime / the frozen war (no internet, the no-fly
zone). · Beat: ordinary world gone; he's on enemy soil and wants out.

**2 · CARE PACKAGE — "The Stake"**
Case: he tries to take the land and sell it; it isn't his until he enrolls. He meets the master. We
learn why he's desperate for the money — broke and finished back in the Union, this is his one shot. ·
Concept: coverage = civil life. · Beat: we're rooting for him.

**3 · ARGUMENT AGAINST TRANSFORMATION — "Blood Money"**
Case: he watches a wrong settled with restitution instead of jail and it disgusts him ("you let him
*buy his way out?*"). · Concept: all crime is civil; restitution, not punishment. · Beat: he plants
his flag against the whole place — "no society works without a state; I'm selling and going home."

**4 · TROUBLE BREWING — "The Clause"**
Case: the sale won't close — his stake binds him under the community contract, and a claim against the
uncle's estate lands on *him*. · Concept: community contracts. · Beat: pulled in, can't leave clean;
the through-line first stirs.

**5 · DOORWAY OF NO RETURN #1 — "All In"**
Case: the Union exit slams shut and he must stay to fight the claim; he enrolls and takes the seat
beside the master. · Concept: first real exposure to the staking table. · Beat: he's *in* — Act 1 ends.

## ACT TWO — learning the world, and the turn  *(expandable teaching band)*

**6 · KICK IN THE SHINS — "Old Reflexes"**
Case: he handles a problem with Union logic and it blows up in his face. · Concept: private security &
agency recovery — *someone comes*, his reflex to "call the authorities" fails. · Beat: humbled; the
rules are real and he doesn't know them.

**7 · (teaching) — "The Tell"**
Case: the master wins a showdown by reading the table when the file says he should lose. · Concept: the
dispute system in full (fold / call / raise / showdown / established–unestablished). · Beat: awe.

**8 · (teaching) — "Sealed"**
Case: a case that turns on the privacy valve and a discreet-witness firm's track record. · Concept:
sealed-by-default + reputation pricing. · Beat: getting fluent, starting to enjoy it.

**9 · (teaching) — "The Debt"**
Case: a man who owes more than he could ever pay, matched to his highest-value work. · Concept: the
work-program / no prisons / restitution beyond means. · Beat: his "barbarism" thesis cracks — it looks
like mercy.

*(Band expandable to taste — each one concept: "Gold" (specie economy), "The Performance" (UN-as-
Eurovision / Dallas-as-Delaware field trip), a Cainland-shadow short. This is where the ~20–23 count
lives.)*

**10 · THE MIRROR MOMENT — "Which Side"**  *(midpoint)*
Case: he defends the system to a visiting Union man, or cracks something the Ancapistan way on pure
instinct, and it frightens him. · Concept: the two world orders (Dallas-as-Delaware) — the
perspective-shift that triggers the mirror. · Beat: *who am I becoming?* He came to sell and leave;
he's putting down roots in the enemy's country.

**11 · PET THE DOG — "Covered"**
Case: he spends his own standing to help a newcomer the system would otherwise chew up. · Concept:
community surety / mutual aid. · Beat: he acts as one of them, by choice — sympathy peak.

**12 · DOORWAY OF NO RETURN #2 — "What Happened to the Uncle"**
Case: he learns the truth about the uncle's death and the Union's hand behind the claim; the endgame
is now inevitable. · Concept: the macro intrudes (cartel route / Union operation — placeholder). ·
Beat: committed; the Union exit is unthinkable now.

---

## ACT THREE — mounting forces, dark night, climax  *(multi-concept)*

**13 · MOUNTING FORCES — "Pressure"**
The adversary bears down on the agency; the heir's Union origin makes him a suspect. · Multi-concept. ·
Beat: back foot — is he a plant?

**14 · LIGHTS OUT — "Going Home"**
His Union past is exposed and weaponized; defecting *back* is dangled as the easy way out; all looks
lost. · Beat: the dark night, the temptation to betray.

**15 · Q FACTOR — "The Letter"**
The callback — the uncle's legacy, the master's faith, the person he's come to love here — that gives
him the strength to choose. · Beat: he decides who he is. (May fold into 14/16.)

**16 · THE FINAL BATTLE — "Showdown"**
The climax case: the agency against the Union-backed threat, at the table, the whole system in play.
The heir supplies the one key only a man of both worlds could, and stands with Ancapistan against his
birth-nation. · Beat: transformation enacted under fire.

**17 · TRANSFORMATION — "Citizen"**
He belongs. The man who came to cash out and run chooses to stay; the letters home invert; the master
makes him a partner. · Beat: the new self, settled — and the series can run again.
